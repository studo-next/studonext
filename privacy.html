Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

At studonext, accessible from our platform, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by studonext and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you directly provide to us when using our tools and interactive modules:

  • Admission & Counseling Forms: When you submit an application (via apply.html or predictor.html), we securely collect your name, email, phone number, academic records, and targeted college preferences to route them to verified counselors via Google Sheets API endpoints.
  • Resume Builder: Any professional history, skills, or contact info you enter into the Resume Generator is parsed entirely client-side or securely cached locally to assemble your layout.
  • Marketplace Listings: Information provided while listing a book (such as title, price, and custom description details) becomes viewable within our community directory.

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the data collected to maintain and optimize your academic experience, specifically to:

  • Process, evaluate, and return metrics via the Advanced AI College Predictor matrix.
  • Improve website operations, prevent script deployment drops, and personalize styling assets.
  • Facilitate counseling connections and dispatch success alerts upon sheet confirmation pipelines.

3. Data Security

The security of your information is extremely important to us. Data submitted through our system uses direct network fetch operations connected to secure web deployment scripts. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we continuously strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

4. Cookies and Web Storage

studonext may use local browser storage or basic cookies to remember configuration inputs (such as saving temporary calculator formulas or resume inputs) to enhance page load performance and prevent data loss during tab refreshes.

5. Third-Party Services

Our infrastructure interacts with Google Services (Google Identity Client Services, Google Fonts, FontAwesome Asset Networks, and Google App Script Web Apps). These platforms operate under their respective independent privacy guidelines.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to reach out through our official WhatsApp support channel connected on the website.
